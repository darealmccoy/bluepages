PROJECTS

HUMMINGBIRD HAVEN - Eagle Scout Project:

  In 2015 - 2016, I led a group of scouts and peers in the construction of a Hummingbird Haven at my local Library The project consisted of laying out the shape of the garden, removing and bagging the top sod layer, planting the garden, and designing and constructing two benches for the library's patrons. In addition, hummingbird feeders were added in order to attract an endangered species of Hummingbird known as the Ruby-throated Hummingbird.
